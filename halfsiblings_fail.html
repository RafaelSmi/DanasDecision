To their horror they lose all their inheritance, luck for them they still have their trust fund money to live off of. However, it bothers them even more that Dana gets everything. This motivates them even more to ruin her. If they couldn't get any of the estate then they definitely won't stand for Dana to get it. They make another plan to get Dana to lose her inheritance as well.

They appealed to Dana's good conscience and guilt her into helping out the step mother. Knowing that Dana, was unaware of the NO-CONTEST clause, due to it being apart of the lawyer's NO-DISCLOSURE. Fortunatley for them when they attempted to put their plan into motion, they were informed that Dana had beat them to it. Once Dana found out of their deceit, she went back to the step mother and helped her to get a share of the estate. Thrilled, they just by and watch as Dana's own generousity ruin her.

It didn't take long for the lawyers to inform Dana of her lost of her inheritance. Unfortunately for her, she had not trustfunf to fall back on like her siblings. Her siblings took such delight in Dana and the stepmother losing everything that they didn't even care about their lost anymore. The selfish half siblings decided to celebrate by taking a trip away on their private jet.
